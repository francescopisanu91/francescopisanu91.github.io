Francesco Pisanu

PhD in Computer Science

Researcher in Computer Science, member of the CORE (Center for Operations Research and Econometrics), at the Université Catholique de Louvain in France, where I moved from University of Sorbonne Paris Nord in Italy in 2020.

I have been teacher at the Institut Galilee.

PhD : On box-total dual integrality and total equimodularity, defended in 2023.

Research

Polyhedral Structures

I am interested in graph theory and polyhedral combinatorics. Most of my efforts are devoted to the study of strong integer properties of polyhedra such as integer decomposition property and box-total dual integrality.

Publications
